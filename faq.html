General Questions

Badekaka is an Agricultural Investment platform that connects farmers with investors funds for agricultural growth and products patnerships. We create a transparent, sustainable ecosystem in providing farmers access partnership funding for their agricultural operations, and in return investors can gain returns of agricultural produce equivalence.

Alot of farmers intend too carry out farming but at some point gets exhausted in finance and resources and that is where Badekaka comes in. Badekaka helps to boastern and strenghten farmers little resources and collaborate with farmers in providing investors funds to achieve a productive harvest season

Step 1: Farmers submit their agricultural projects and colaborating funding requirements.
Step 2: Investors funds at the equivalent product value of agricultural produce are given out to the farmer.
Step 3: Farmers use these funds for farm top-up operations (seeds, fertilizers, labor, equipment).
Step 4: At harvest, farmers return the agreed quantity of produce in equivalent of investor fund distributed.
Result: Investors receive produce, farmers retain surplus for profit.

For Investors

1. Create an account on our platform
2. Complete identity verification
3. Browse available farming projects
4. Select a project to invest in
5. Fund the investment (equivalent to produce value)
6. Monitor your investment and receive returns at harvest

The minimum investment is equivalent to the value of one bag of agricultural produce (typically starting from ₦30,000 per bag return for rice). However, this may vary by crop and season. Check the specific project details for minimum requirements.

Returns depend on market prices at harvest time. Historical data shows:

• 2025: Returns continued with strong market rally
Note: Past performance doesn't guarantee future results.

You receive your returns in the form of agricultural produce agreed upon in your investment contract. You can either:

• Store it through our partnered storage facilities

Our recovery rate has been consistently high:

For Farmers

1. Reach Out to our field agent through the contacts provided in our contact page
2. Submit your farming project details (crop type, land size, funding needed)
3. Provide required documentation (ID, land proof, farming history)
4. Wait for farm verification and assessment
5. Once approved, your project is listed for investors
6. Receive funding and begin operations

Currently, we support major staple crops like rice. We are expanding sooner to include more crops such as maize, beans, and cassava based on market demand and investor interest. Check our platform for the complete list of supported crops in your region.

Funding amount depends on:
• Size of your farm
• Type of crop you're growing
• Your farming history and experience
• Current market prices
Most farmers receive funding equivalent to 5-10 bags of produce. Contact our team for a personalized assessment.

Repayment is simple: return the agreed quantity of produce at harvest time. You don't repay in cash. For example:
• You receive ₦150,000
• You agree to return 5 bags of rice at harvest ie ₦30,000 per bag ROI
• After harvest, deliver the 5 bags to the investor
• You keep all surplus harvest for profit
